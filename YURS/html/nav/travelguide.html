Things to consider

UAE covid status as of March 22, 2022:

Level 3: COVID-19 High



- Make sure you are vaccinated and up to date with your COVID-19 vaccines before traveling to United Arab Emirates. If you are not up to date with COVID-19 vaccines, avoid travel to United Arab Emirates.

- PCR test with negative test results is also a must if you are planning to traveling in the UAE.

- If requested, passengers must undergo a COVID-19 RT-PCR test on arrival in Dubai and quarantine themselves until they receive a negative result.

Important Rules in the UAE to Remember

- Photography of certain government buildings and military installations isn’t allowed. Don’t photograph people without their permission.


- Bringing pork is illegal in the UAE as it’s a muslim country


- For alhcohol, Dubai is the only emirate where you need to have liquor license to drink alchohol,tourists are able to obtain a temporary liquor licence for the duration of a month from the two official liquor distributors in Dubai. Tourists will be provided with a code of conduct document and will be asked to confirm they understand rules and regulations in relation to purchasing, transporting and consuming liquor in Dubai. Other emirates is fine with buying alchohol for personal consumption. BUT ONLY SHARJAH STRICTLY PROHIBITS THE PURCHASE AND CONSUMPTION OF ALCHOHOL.


Another thing that you should be aware that it is a punishable offence under UAE law to drink or be under the influence of alcohol in public, so you should only drink alchohol in licensed venues or at your home.



- Women should dress modestly when in public areas like shopping malls. Clothes should cover the tops of the arms and legs, and underwear should not be visible. Swimming attire should be worn only on beaches or at swimming pools.
