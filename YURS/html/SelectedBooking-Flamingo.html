Set in a tropical garden in Umm Al Quwain, Flamingo Beach Resort offers views of the mangrove islands, a private beach area, an outdoor pool. Umm Al Quwain Museum is a 10-minute walk away. Free WiFi is available throughout the entire property.

All rooms at Flamingo Beach Resort face the pool, the lagoon and the mangrove islands beyond. Some have a seating area with a sofa and each is air-conditioned. Guests can enjoy many water activities such as crab hunting, snorkeling, and Flamingo tours.
