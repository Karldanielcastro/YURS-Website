Yas Waterworld

Yas Waterworld reflects traditional Emirati architecture in the entirety of the waterpark’s design. It includes a detailed authenticity to historic Emirati heritage with an Arabian castle-like exterior design, and a village-like interior design. The conception and engineering of the Emirati fantasy theme waterpark was built on the narrative of The Legend of the Lost Pearl, which is inspired by Emirati culture and history, and is obvious in the waterpark’s architecture, attractions, and thrills. Its pearl is seen from outside the waterpark, and all the characters, attractions, shops and restaurants are also based on the story.
