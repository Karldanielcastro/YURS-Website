Al Badayer, Sharjah, United Arab Emirates

Book now

Situated in Sharjah, 43 km from Sharjah National Park, Mysk Al Badayer Retreat features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a garden. With free WiFi, this 5-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk and room service. The hotel offers an indoor pool and a concierge service.

Continental and buffet breakfast options are available each morning at the hotel. Mysk Al Badayer Retreat offers a terrace.
