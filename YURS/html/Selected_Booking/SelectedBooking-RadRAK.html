Al Jazeera Marjan Island, Ras al Khaimah, United Arab Emirates

Book now

This property is 3 minutes walk from the beach. Set in Ras al Khaimah, 3.2 km from Al Hamra Mall, Radisson Resort Ras Al Khaimah Marjan Island offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre. Featuring a bar, the 4-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.

At the hotel, all rooms have a desk, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linen and towels. Rooms are fitted with a safety deposit box, while some rooms also offer a balcony. All guest rooms feature a wardrobe.

Continental and buffet breakfast options are available every morning at Radisson Resort Ras Al Khaimah.


The accommodation offers a sun terrace. The area is popular for cycling, and car hire is available at Radisson Resort Ras Al Khaimah Marjan Island.
