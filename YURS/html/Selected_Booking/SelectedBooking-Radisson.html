The brand new, centrally located Radisson Blu Hotel, offers stylish interiors, wide array of restaurants and bars and an outdoor swimming pool. Guests can enjoy free high-speed WiFi throughout the property.

All 148 rooms and suites are stylishly decorated and come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and Apple TV. Enjoy a cup of tea while looking out at the pool or city. Rooms include a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find slippers, free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Among its 6 restaurants and bars you will find a signature restaurant: Filini - simply cooked Italian food crafted from carefully chosen ingredients.


Relaxation and rejuvenation is never far away with the elegant spa facilities having private massage rooms and a temperature controlled swimming pool.


The 4 meeting rooms and large ballroom come fully equipped with stylishly sophisticated solutions for memorable and successful meetings, weddings and events.
