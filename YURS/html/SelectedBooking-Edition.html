Al Bateen Marina, Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates

Book now

Abu Dhabi EDITION is situated at the Al Bateen Marina in dowtown Abu Dhabi. The 5-star hotel boasts two pools, restaurants, conference facilities and a three-story nightclub.

The air-conditioned rooms and apartments feature a a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with luxury amenities. Some of the units have a balcony and views over the marina.

The hotel has 2 signature restaurants, a gourmet market, and guests can enjoy drinks at the pool or lobby bar.


Guests can also take advantage of the state-of-the-art fitness centre or relax at the spa which offers 7 treatment rooms.
